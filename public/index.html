Career

  • December 13, 1989 Taylor Alison Swift was born in West Reading, Pennsylvania.
  • 2003 at age 14, she moved to Nashville to become a country artist.
  • 2004 she signed a songwriting deal with Sony/ATV Music Publishing.
  • 2005 she signed a recording contract with Big Machine Records
  • 2006 her self-titled debut album made her the first female country singer to write a U.S. platinum-certified album.
